General Summary
==================================================
The main goal of the document is to provide detailed data on uranium marketing activities in the United States from 2018 through 2023, with summary data dating back to 2002. The report is based on information reported on Form EIA-858, the Uranium Marketing Annual Survey, which collects data on contracts, deliveries, enrichment services purchased, inventories, and other related aspects of the uranium market. The report is prepared by the U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA), an independent statistical and analytical agency within the U.S. Department of Energy, and aims to provide stakeholders with valuable insights into the uranium market trends and dynamics.
